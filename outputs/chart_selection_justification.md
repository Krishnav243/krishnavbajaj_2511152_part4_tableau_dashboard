# Chart Selection Justification

## Sales Trend Over Time

### Chart Type

Line Chart

### Reason

A line chart is the most effective visualization for displaying changes in sales over time. It allows users to identify trends, growth patterns, and seasonality.

---

## Regional Performance

### Chart Type

Bar Chart

### Reason

Bar charts provide a clear comparison of sales across regions and allow users to quickly identify top-performing and underperforming regions.

---

## Category Profitability

### Chart Type

Bar Chart

### Reason

Bar charts enable direct comparison of profitability across product categories and make ranking categories straightforward.

---

## Customer Segment Analysis

### Chart Type

Bar Chart

### Reason

Bar charts effectively compare sales performance among customer segments and support quick interpretation.

---

## Discount vs Profit

### Chart Type

Scatter Plot

### Reason

Scatter plots are ideal for analyzing relationships between two numerical variables and help identify trends, clusters, and outliers.

---

## Shipping Performance

### Chart Type

Bar Chart

### Reason

Bar charts clearly compare average delivery times across shipping methods and support operational analysis.

---

## Return Analysis

### Chart Type

Bar Chart

### Reason

Bar charts provide an intuitive comparison of return rates across product categories and make problem areas easy to identify.

---

## KPI Cards

### Visualization Type

Text KPI Cards

### Reason

KPI cards provide an immediate overview of critical business metrics and support executive decision-making.

---

## Dashboard Filters

### Filters Used

* Region
* Category
* Customer Segment
* Order Date

### Reason

Interactive filters allow users to perform targeted analysis and improve dashboard usability.

---

## Dashboard Actions

### Action Type

Filter Action

### Reason

Filter actions enhance interactivity by allowing users to click on a region and automatically update all related visualizations.
