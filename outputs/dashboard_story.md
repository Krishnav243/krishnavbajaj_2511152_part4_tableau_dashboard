# Dashboard Story

## Objective

The purpose of this dashboard is to provide executives and business managers with a comprehensive view of sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, returns, and shipping efficiency.

---

## Dashboard Components

### KPI Section

The dashboard begins with three key performance indicators:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Return Rate

These KPIs provide a high-level summary of overall business performance.

---

### Sales Trend Analysis

The Sales Trend chart visualizes monthly sales performance and helps identify growth patterns, seasonal fluctuations, and periods of high demand.

---

### Regional Performance Analysis

The Regional Performance chart compares sales across regions and highlights regional differences in business performance.

---

### Category Profitability Analysis

The Category Profitability chart evaluates how product categories contribute to sales and profits.

---

### Customer Segment Analysis

This visualization compares sales performance across different customer segments and helps identify valuable customer groups.

---

### Discount vs Profit Analysis

The scatter plot examines the relationship between discount levels and profitability.

---

### Shipping Performance Analysis

Shipping Performance helps assess delivery efficiency by comparing average delivery times across shipping modes.

---

### Return Analysis

Return Analysis identifies product categories with high return rates and highlights potential operational issues.

---

## Interactivity

The dashboard includes interactive filters for:

* Region
* Category
* Customer Segment
* Order Date

Users can dynamically explore the data and focus on specific business areas.

Additionally, selecting a region automatically filters all related visualizations through dashboard actions.

---

## Conclusion

The dashboard serves as an executive decision-support tool that enables users to monitor performance, identify opportunities, and make data-driven business decisions.
